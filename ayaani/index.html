Mission

Our mission at Ayaani diamonds is to make affordable lab grown diamond jewellery for the poised, elegant, and sophisticated woman who wants to sparkle on her big day and beyond

Vision

Ayaani Diamonds adds the word affordable and ethical to lab grown diamonds apart from the classic four C’s – cut, color, carat, and clarity.

We are committed to making the purest jewellery made in labs that are physically, optically, and chemically similar to natural diamonds. Our designs, style, and ethically grown diamonds set us apart from everyone. The various jewellery designs exude unmatched jubilance, glow, and shine – just like all the beautiful women out there.

We adhere to the Quality-Check approach at Ayaani Diamonds, which means our professionals check every diamond for quality, resulting in elegant and dazzling jewellery that every woman desires as part of her mighty jewellery collection.
